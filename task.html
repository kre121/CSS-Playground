My favorite food

Ingredients

Ingredient Amount
bigger champignons 3
bluecheese 60g
minced ham 150g

Instructions

  1. Step 1 Clean the mushrooms and prepare the oven, preheat the oven to 200°C
  2. Step 2 Fry the minced ham and add to empty mushrooms, add bluecheese, grated if needed
  3. Step 3 Put them in the oven, wait 15 minutes and they are ready to eat!
